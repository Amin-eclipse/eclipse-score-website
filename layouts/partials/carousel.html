From Proprietary To Open Source

The industry has long tackled software challenges independently – often duplicating efforts and building closed, incompatible systems.
S-CORE changes this by uniting OEMs, suppliers and developers around a common SW-platform.
The goal of S-CORE is to reduce complexity, promote re-use, gain speed & enable scalable innovation.
